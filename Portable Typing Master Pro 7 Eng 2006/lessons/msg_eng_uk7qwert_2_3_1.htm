TypingMaster Satellite is a personal learning assistant. It tracks your typing when you use other programs, collects data and provides training for those keys and words that are difficult for you.

The Satellite will be most helpful once you have completed this course and start using your new typing skills on daily basis. At that point, the Satellite will continue to efficiently hone your skills.

However, you can already activate the Satellite to see how it works. You can try out the training features, start recording real-world typing statistics and use the Take-a-Break Reminder.

To use the Satellite:
1. Click 'OK'
2. Choose 'Satellite' from the menu on the right
3. Follow the instructions on screen to launch the Satellite

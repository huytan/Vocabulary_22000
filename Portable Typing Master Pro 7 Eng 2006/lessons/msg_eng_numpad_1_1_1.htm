The numeric keypad, also called 10-key pad, is a very effective and fast tool for keying in series of numbers.

In this three lesson course, you will learn to use the numeric keypad efficiently with touch typing technique.

Data entry speed is often measured in Keystrokes per Hour (KPH). To convert Words per Minute (WPM) to KPH simply multiply the WPM result by 300.

Alternatively, if you have set TypingMaster speed unit to Keystrokes per Minute (KPM), you can multiply the KPM result by 60.

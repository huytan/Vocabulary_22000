Increasing your typing speed requires regular practice. In addition to your daily typing, you can complete this six lesson course for extra training.

In the first lesson, you will review the home row keys and type sentences focusing on them.

In the next four lessons, you will review the upper and lower extension rows for all fingers. You will also type sentences that put emphasis on one finger at a time.

In the last lesson, you will train typing some of the most common words in English and words that are often misspelled.

Note: The course is designed for the QWERTY keyboard. If you are using a keyboard other than QWERTY, the keys to review are not necessarily on the same finger. This especially if using the DVORAK keyboard.
